Know Your Rights

Consumer Rights

Right to safety against hazardous goods, to be informed about quality and price, to choose from a variety of goods, and to seek redressal against unfair trade practices.

Employee Rights

Right to equal pay for equal work, protection against workplace harassment, right to form unions, and entitlement to social security benefits like provident fund and gratuity.

Fundamental Rights

Right to equality, freedom of speech and expression, right to life and personal liberty, and the right to constitutional remedies for the enforcement of these rights.

Right to Information

Right of any citizen to request information from a public authority, promoting transparency and accountability in the working of every public authority.

Digital & Cyber Rights

Includes the right to data privacy, protection from online harassment, and freedom of speech on the internet, governed by the IT Act and data protection laws.

Rights of Arrested Person

Right to be informed of the grounds of arrest, right to consult a legal practitioner of choice, and the right to be produced before a magistrate within 24 hours.
